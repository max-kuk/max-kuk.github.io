About Me

Salut! I'm Maksim Kukushkin, a graduated from Leipzig University. I am delighted to be working as a Research Assistant in the Machine Learning Group at the Neuromorphic Information Processing Department of Leipzig University. Currently, I am working on analyzing hyperspectral data and developing algorithms for classification of various seed species.

Research Interests

My research centers on computer vision with a specialization in hyperspectral imaging. Specifically, I am interested in various aspects of classification, anomaly detection, and morphological networks for hyperspectral and multispectral imaging.
